## 💡 Using Key Features

This version focuses on three primary tools to speed up your editing workflow.

### Timeline Editing
Place your video clips on the timeline to create a sequence. You can drag clips in any order. The snapping function helps you align edges of clips without gaps. You can trim clips by hovering over the start or end points and dragging them inward.

### AI Object Masking
The software includes tools to track objects in your frame automatically. Select the masking tool in the effect controls panel. You then draw a shape over the object you want to isolate. Premiere Pro tracks the movement of that object frame by frame. This tool is useful for blurring faces or changing the color of specific items.

### Color Correction
The Lumetri Color panel allows you to adjust the look of your footage. Use the basic correction tab to fix white balance and exposure. You can adjust shadows, midtones, and highlights with precise sliders. Premiere Pro also provides scopes that show color balance data to keep your image consistent.

## 🔧 Managing Hardware Settings

5. Click OK to save your changes.

## 📁 Importing Your Media

To bring your video files into a project, use the project panel:

1. Right-click the empty space in the project panel.
2. Select Import.
3. Choose the files from your computer storage.
4. Click Open.
5. Your files appear in the bin.

## 📤 Exporting Your Project

When you finish your edit, save your work as a video file:

1. Select the timeline sequence.
2. Open the File menu.
3. Select Export.
4. Choose the Media option.
5. Select a format like H.264 for web compatibility.
6. Choose a destination folder on your drive.
7. Click Export to create the final video file.

## 💬 Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if the software crashes?
Ensure your graphics card drivers are current. Check the manufacturer website for your card to download the latest updates.

Can I move the interface panels?
Yes. Drag the name of any panel to a new position on the screen. The interface will highlight where the panel fits.

How do I clear the cache?
Go to Preferences, then select Media Cache. Click the Remove button to delete temporary files that the software creates during use. This can improve drive space.
